Health and security

In the case that a resident of Spain has the need to go abroad due to force majeure, it is necessary that they have filled out a mandatory form to be granted permission to travel and not cause problems due to the pandemic and maintain the safety of all. In this form you will only be asked for information about the person for informational purposes. The information will not be shared with people outside the association.
