Legal information

Each passenger must answer a form associated with each trip, personal and non-transferable. Make sure you fill out this form and sign it before flying to Spain. And remember that, after signing the form, you will receive a QR code associated with your trip, which you must bring on your mobile or printed on paper in order to pass the airport health control. YOU MUST NOT TRAVEL if you have symptoms compatible with COVID-19 (fever, cough, shortness of breath), of recent onset, if you have been diagnosed with COVID-19 in the last 14 days or if you have had close contact with a confirmed case of COVID- 19 in the last two weeks.
