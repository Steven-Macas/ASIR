The negativity and security of Burgos in Castilla y León.

At the beginning of all this Burgos fell into an abyss causing infections and deaths to skyrocket on the table. After a first contact and being able to regulate
all this problem it was not going to be so easy. The Community reaches 951 cases per 100,000 inhabitants in 14 days and continues to be at extreme risk. Burgos has a surface area of 107.06 km², with 175,821 inhabitants. Which makes it the second most populated province in the autonomous community and it was the one that was taking it the worst.
